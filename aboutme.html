Love with Data!

My first encounter with data sparked when I was just a child, sitting in front of the TV, engrossed in a cricket match. As I watched the scoreboard, my young eyes lit up with curiosity, and I found myself instinctively counting every run, wicket, and statistic. What began as a simple fascination soon blossomed into a deep love affair with data, and the numbers on that cricket scoreboard became the foundation of my enduring passion for all things data-related.

My love affair with data was revealed to me during a seemingly ordinary day at college as an adult. I found myself engrossed in a spreadsheet during the project, diving deep into rows and columns of numbers, searching for patterns, trends, and insights. As I lost track of time, it suddenly struck me that I was genuinely passionate about this process. That very moment, I realized that I had fallen head over heels for the world of data, and I knew I had found my true calling.
